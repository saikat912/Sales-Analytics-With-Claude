# American Eagle Seasonal Marketing Strategy Playbook

## Objective

Maximize revenue and profitability during the two strongest demand
periods identified in the sales analysis: - **Back-to-School (Q2/Q3
seasonal ramp)** - **Holiday Season (Q4)**

This playbook provides a step-by-step execution guide for the marketing
team.

## 1. Define Success Metrics (8--12 weeks before campaign)

**Primary KPIs** - Revenue - Operating profit - Units sold - Conversion
rate - Average order value (AOV) - Return on ad spend (ROAS) - Customer
acquisition cost (CAC) - Email/SMS revenue contribution - Repeat
purchase rate

## 2. Customer Segmentation

Prioritize: 1. Existing loyalty members 2. High-value repeat customers
3. Lapsed customers (inactive 6--12 months) 4. New shoppers (Gen Z,
college students, parents)

Personalize messaging by customer segment, geography, and purchase
history.

## 3. Back-to-School Campaign (Launch 6--8 Weeks Early)

### Goals

-   Increase traffic before competitors.
-   Grow basket size.
-   Acquire new customers before classes begin.

### Actions

-   Launch themed collections and campus essentials.
-   Bundle complementary products (jeans + tops + accessories).
-   Offer student discounts and referral incentives.
-   Partner with creators on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.
-   Use countdown campaigns highlighting limited-time offers.

### Weekly Execution

-   Weeks 1--2: Awareness
-   Weeks 3--4: Influencer content + social ads
-   Weeks 5--6: Retarget abandoned carts
-   Weeks 7--8: Scarcity messaging and final promotions

## 4. Holiday Campaign (Launch 8--10 Weeks Early)

### Goals

-   Maximize peak-season revenue.
-   Increase gift purchases.
-   Improve repeat purchases after Christmas.

### Actions

-   Publish holiday gift guides.
-   Offer tiered promotions instead of blanket discounts.
-   Create premium bundles to increase AOV.
-   Expand paid search, social, and email frequency.
-   Prepare Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas, and New Year
    promotions separately.
-   Promote gift cards and last-minute digital gifts.

### Promotional Calendar

-   Early November: Gift discovery
-   Black Friday/Cyber Monday: Best offers
-   Early December: Premium bundles
-   Mid-December: Shipping urgency
-   Post-Christmas: Gift-card redemption
-   New Year: Clearance and loyalty offers

## 5. Omnichannel Strategy

Coordinate messaging across: - Website - Mobile app - Email - SMS -
Instagram - TikTok - Facebook - Retail stores

Ensure consistent creative, pricing, and offers.

## 6. Personalization

-   Dynamic product recommendations.
-   Personalized coupons.
-   Cart recovery emails.
-   Browse-abandonment campaigns.
-   Loyalty-exclusive early access.

## 7. Inventory & Operations

Marketing should align with merchandising to: - Forecast demand. -
Prioritize high-margin inventory. - Prevent stockouts. - Shift inventory
between regions. - Monitor fulfillment capacity daily.

## 8. Measurement Cadence

Review every week: - Revenue vs target - ROAS by channel - Conversion
rate - AOV - Units sold - Inventory sell-through - Margin by category

Reallocate budget toward the highest-performing channels and audiences.

## 9. Post-Campaign Review

Within two weeks after each season: - Compare results against KPIs. -
Identify best-performing products and campaigns. - Document lessons
learned. - Build audiences for the next seasonal campaign.

## Implementation Timeline

  Time Before Peak   Activity
  ------------------ ----------------------------------------------
  10--12 weeks       Planning, forecasting, creative concepts
  8--10 weeks        Audience segmentation and content production
  6--8 weeks         Launch awareness campaigns
  4--6 weeks         Increase paid media and influencer activity
  2--4 weeks         Promotions, retargeting, urgency messaging
  Peak weeks         Daily optimization and inventory monitoring
  1--2 weeks after   Performance review and insights

## Expected Business Impact

-   Higher seasonal revenue through earlier demand generation.
-   Increased average order value via bundles and premium offers.
-   Improved operating margins by prioritizing profitable categories.
-   Stronger customer retention through loyalty and personalization.
-   More efficient marketing spend using continuous KPI optimization.
